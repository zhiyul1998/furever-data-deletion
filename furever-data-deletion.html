Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data associated with the Furever app, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to support@fureverapp.com with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include the following information in your email:
    • Your name
    • The email address or account ID associated with your app account
  3. We will process your request and delete your data within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

If you have any further questions, feel free to contact us at support@fureverapp.com.
